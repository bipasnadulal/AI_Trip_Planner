This is the Trip Planner agent which uses Gemini-2.5-Flash Model.
The agent’s purpose is to plan a day trip based on the
- user’s mood, interests, and budget.
The agent uses the Google Search tool to gather real-time information.
The agent has several capabilities:
- Budget awareness – suggests activities that match the user’s budget (cheap or splurge).
- Mood matching – adjusts recommendations depending on the user’s mood (relaxing, adventurous, etc.).
- Real-time information – finds current information about venues and events using Google Search.
- Daily planning – organizes the trip into morning, afternoon, and evening activities.

Input From the User:
- mood
- interests
- budget

Output From the Agent:
- a schedule divided into morning, afternoon, and evening
- recommended places to visit
- links and opening hours for venues
- activities that match the user’s budget
